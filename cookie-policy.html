Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026  ·  Effective: April 18, 2026

We keep our cookie usage minimal and purposeful. This policy explains exactly what cookies Venturli uses, why we use them, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — like whether you're logged in — so you don't have to re-enter it every time. Cookies can also include similar technologies like local storage and session storage.

Cookies are widely used and not inherently harmful. They do not contain executable code and cannot access other files on your device.

2. How Venturli Uses Cookies

Venturli uses a minimal set of cookies focused on making the platform work correctly. We do not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data to third parties.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
sb-auth-token Essential Keeps you logged in to your Venturli account via Supabase authentication Session / 1 week
sb-refresh-token Essential Refreshes your login session so you stay authenticated without logging in repeatedly 1 week
theme-preference Functional Remembers your light/dark mode preference across sessions 1 year
stripe-mid Essential Set by Stripe during payment processing for fraud prevention and security 1 year
__vercel_live_token Functional Set by Vercel for deployment preview functionality (only present in preview environments) Session

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential cookies are necessary for the platform to function. Without them you cannot log in, make bookings, or use core features. These cannot be disabled without breaking the platform.

Functional cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences. They are not strictly required but improve usability. You can disable these through your browser settings.

We currently do not use analytics cookies or advertising cookies. If we introduce these in the future, we will update this policy and obtain your consent where required.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on the Venturli platform are set by third-party services we use to deliver our product:

  • Supabase — sets authentication cookies to manage your login session. See Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — sets cookies during payment flows for security and fraud prevention. See Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — may set cookies related to hosting and deployment infrastructure. See Vercel's privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking or advertising services at this time.

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Venturli uses browser local storage to save certain app state on your device — such as your current trip view, UI preferences, and cached data. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

6. How to Control Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Set preferences for future cookie use

Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using the Venturli platform.

7. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Because we do not engage in cross-site tracking, our platform responds the same way regardless of whether a DNT signal is present.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our use of cookies evolves. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. For material changes, we may also notify you by email or through the app.
